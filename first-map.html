My Summer Vacation: Spain and Portugal edition

by Micaela Leiva

Over the summer, I visited Spain and Portugal with my parents. Our flight landed in Madrid so that was our first stop. We only stayed for one night because we were going to visit my Aunt who lives in Marbella, which is located on the southern part of Spain. After visiting for three days, we drove all the way from Marbella to Portugal, Spain's neighbouring country. We visited the beautiful cities of Algarve, Lisbon, and Porto. After six long days, we returned to Madrid to fly back to the United States.
