Historical Places in Buenos Aires

by Micaela Leiva

The city of Buenos Aires has a lot of history which can be deduced by the amount of historical buildings it posseses. From its beautiful architecture to thousands of historical sites, Buenos Aires has a lot to offer both to tourists and its people. Buenos Aires got its independence from Spain in 1816 and its history grew a lot from there. Also, it received a significant amount of european immigrants towards the end of the 1800s until the beginning of the 1900s. As a city that is so rich in many different cultures, Buenos Aires can be considered an incredibly diverse city, historically speaking.
